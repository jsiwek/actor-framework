enum cppa::intrusive::enqueue_result
strong

Denotes in which state queue and reader are after an enqueue.

Enumerator
success 

Indicates that the enqueue operation succeeded and the reader is ready to receive the data.

unblocked_reader 

Indicates that the enqueue operation succeeded and the reader is currently blocked, i.e., needs to be re-scheduled.

queue_closed 

Indicates that the enqueue operation failed because the queue has been closed by the reader.
